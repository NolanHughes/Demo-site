Hughes Brews

Delicious Homebrewed Beer

Hughes Brews is a husband and wife brewing team that has been making delicious beer since 2015.

They brew 5 gallon batches and have a whole lot of fun doing it. Jessie and Nolan's taste in beer differs slightly, but they haven't made anything yet that they didn't enjoy.
